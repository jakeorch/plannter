About grade level difficulty

Grade level difficulty is calculated using the difficulty of individual courses, the number of courses, and comparing it to the average number of courses taken.

It does not take the subjects of individual courses into account.

About grade level difficulty percent

Grade level difficulty percent is the percentage of the ratio of advanced classes to total classes.

It does not take the difficulty of individual courses into account.
