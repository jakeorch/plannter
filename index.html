About grade level difficulty

Grade level difficulty is calculated using the advancement level and difficulty of individual courses, the number of courses, and compares it to the average number of courses taken.

About grade level difficulty percent

Grade level difficulty percent is the percentage of the ratio of advanced classes to total classes.

It does not take the advancement level or difficulty of individual courses into account.
